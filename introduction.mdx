## What is ReactUI Email?

ReactUI Email is a collection of professional email templates built on top of [React Email](https://react.email) and fully styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com). It provides an easy-to-use and customizable solution for creating production-ready email templates in React.

<Info>
  ReactUI Email leverages the power of React components and Tailwind CSS to help you build beautiful emails that work across all major email clients.
</Info>

## Who is this for?

ReactUI Email is perfect for:

- **Developers** building SaaS applications who need professional transactional emails
- **Product teams** looking for ready-to-use email templates that can be quickly customized
- **Startups** wanting to ship high-quality email experiences without designing from scratch
- **Anyone** who prefers working with React components and Tailwind CSS over traditional HTML email templates

## Available templates

ReactUI Email includes templates for common email scenarios:

- Welcome emails
- Email verification
- Password reset and account security
- Product updates and newsletters
- Team invitations
- Login alerts
- And many more

Each template is fully customizable and can be adapted to match your brand identity.

<Tip>
  All templates use the `@react-email/components` library under the hood, giving you access to reliable, battle-tested email components.
</Tip>
